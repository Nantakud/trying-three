Welcome to my experiment with THREE

I like learning new stuff. 3d is super cool

The first object is a torus, or better a giant donuts. No icing on top, but it is very red. It has two light pointed over it, and it moves not too quickly in three directions.

The second item is an icosahedron. It has 20 faces, and it rembembers me of some dices I used to do role playing. To style it a little bit, I added a normal map with floreal patterns, and a question mark. A orange light from above completes the creation. It obviously rotates.

The third object is a torus too, but this time around it comes in a knot fashion. I made it pink, to match the stars coloro and it looks like a giant candy.

I just started with a youtube tutorial, and added some twists here and there. Not too enthusiast of the result, but it's fair enough as fist attempt. I think I'm gonna try something more extreme next time Thanks for watching, I hope you enjoyed
